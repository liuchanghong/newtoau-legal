About This Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how the AusRights mobile application ("AusRights", "the app", "we" or "us") handles information when you use the app.

Information We Collect

AusRights does not require an account and does not collect personal information from you. The app does not use advertising, analytics or tracking technologies.

Information Stored on Your Device

When you select a display language, the app saves that language preference locally on your device so that it can remember your selection. This preference is not sent to us or to any third party by the app. You can change it at any time in Settings, and removing the app removes its locally stored data.

External Websites and Phone Calls

AusRights includes links to official Australian government and agency websites and telephone numbers for relevant services. If you choose to open a link or make a call, your interaction takes place outside AusRights and is subject to the privacy practices of the relevant website, service provider or telecommunications carrier. AusRights does not receive information about those interactions.

This Privacy Policy Website

This policy page is intended to be hosted using GitHub Pages. When you visit this web page, GitHub may process technical information in accordance with the GitHub General Privacy Statement. The page itself does not include analytics, advertising or tracking scripts operated by AusRights.

Children's Privacy

AusRights does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Data Disclosure and Sale

Because AusRights does not collect personal information, we do not sell personal information or disclose it to third parties through the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the features or privacy practices of AusRights change. The effective date above will be updated when a revised policy is published.
