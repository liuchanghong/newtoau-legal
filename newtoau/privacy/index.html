Privacy Policy

Updated 5 Jun 2026

NewToAU is an informational app for people who are new to Australia. This policy explains how the app handles privacy and data.

Information we collect

NewToAU does not require an account and does not ask you to provide personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, date of birth or identity documents.

The app does not collect analytics, advertising identifiers, precise location, contacts, photos, health data or financial account information.

Information stored on your device

NewToAU stores some app preferences locally on your device so the app can work as expected. This may include:

  • Your selected state or territory
  • Your selected visa or residency type
  • Your selected app language
  • Checklist progress, saved guides and recent search history

This information is stored locally using standard iOS storage. It is used only to personalise the app experience on your device.

Tracking and advertising

NewToAU does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies. The app does not include third-party advertising SDKs.

External websites

NewToAU includes links to official Australian government and other authoritative websites. When you choose to open an external website, you leave NewToAU and the privacy practices of that external website apply.

Children

NewToAU is designed as a general information app. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

General information only

NewToAU provides general information only. It is not legal, migration, tax, financial or medical advice. Always check the linked official source or contact the relevant agency or a qualified professional before making important decisions.
